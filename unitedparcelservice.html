United Parcel service

Loader

I've been at UPS for 4 years now this June. I started out as a loader, loading the 53 foot trailers. This was easily the hardest job I have ever had. I started in the summer and I was not prepared for how physically demanding this job would be! The trailers sit in the sun and heat up all day and it can easily pass 120 degrees inside the trailers with no air conditioning, it was literally like a sauna. I would regularly handle over 1000 packages in a day and leave drenched in sweat. I loaded trailers for over a year before I was promoted.

Supervisor

After loading for a year I was offered a promotion to become a supervisor, with a huge raise, and no physical requirements I was all for it. this job has taught me the most out of any job thus far. I lead a team of 11-14 guys every day and over see them loading the outbound trailers. I ensure that all trailers leave on time, and that all employees are working safely and productively. I'm at the point where I'm deciding if I want to continue with UPS or switch careers.
