Brazilian Jui jitsu

Halo BJJ

Around spring of 2018 I started taking Brazlian Jui Jitsu classes at Halo BJJ Academy. This gym is located on Chestnut and Ashlan across from Blackbeard's. BJJ is a martial art that focuses on self defense, grappling, and submissions. Although I don't train as much as I wish I could due to work and school, I still manage to get in a few sessions a month. BJJ gave me a ton of confidence knowing that I could defend myself if I needed too. I also love to compete and I competed in my first tournament in my first year of training. I took gold, winning 3 out 3 matches! I highly recommend anyone struggling with confidence issues to give BJJ a try.
